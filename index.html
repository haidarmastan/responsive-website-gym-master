About
STAR Martial Arts & Fitness Club

STAR Club is a martial arts academy and fully-equipped strength training facility with a reputation for excellence in combact sports since our founding in 1969. Our team of coaches are experienced athletes, award-winning competitors, certified professionals and elite presenters in their fields. World class Muay Thai, Brazilian JiuJitsu, Judo and Pekiti Tirsia Kali classes take place on our extensive and well-maintained mat area.

Mixed martial arts (MMA) is a full-contact combat sport that allows both striking and grappling, both standing and on the ground, using effective techniques from other combat sports and martial arts. Melding the most effective techniques from all martial arts, MMA gives a real world feel to the competition realm. Kickboxing, boxing, Brazilian jiu jitsu, karate, judo, kung fu, wrestling and countless other arts have been infused and evolve MMA daily. Competition, self defense, discipline and countless other benefits come from training this art.
